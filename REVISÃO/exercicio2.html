Cristiano Ronaldo

Portuguese Forward (Age: 37)

Cristiano Ronaldo dos Santos Aveiro (Portugal, born 5 February 1985) is a Portuguese professional footballer who plays as a forward for Saudi Professional League club Al Nassr and captains the Portugal national team. Widely regarded as one of the greatest players of all time.
